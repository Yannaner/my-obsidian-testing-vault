# Daily Notes

My daily thoughts and reflections.

## Why Keep Daily Notes?

Daily notes help you:

- Track your progress over time
- Reflect on what you've learned
- Build a personal knowledge base
- Develop writing habits

## My Daily Note Template

```markdown
# [Date]

## What I did today
-

## What I learned
-

## Ideas and thoughts
-

## Tomorrow's plan
-
```

## Tips for Success

1. **Be consistent**: Write every day, even if it's just a few sentences
2. **Don't overthink**: Raw thoughts are valuable too
3. **Review periodically**: Look back at old notes to see patterns
4. **Link liberally**: Connect ideas across different days
